CAN I ORDER TAKEOUT OR SIMPLY PICK UP THE FOOD?

Of course! We're a tiny restaurant, so takeout is a way for us to serve more people the good food they deserve. Just call 123–456–7890 for advance orders.

HOW OFTEN DO YOU CHANGE THE MENU?

We typically change every quarter, to coincide with the changes in seasonal produce. We make everything fresh, so this is crucial for us. However, our Sumptuous Staples menu stays the same all year.
